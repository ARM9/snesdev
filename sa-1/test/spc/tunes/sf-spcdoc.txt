	Star fox spc engine doc
Unlike some games, star fox does NOT store all sound data in ARAM at once.
Some sound effect samples seem to be uploaded to ARAM during screen transitions depending on level/menu.

	APU IO ports

$2140
Command port
Write:
$F1 - Fade out tune
$F2 - Reduces master volume drastically to a set value, like when pausing a game.
$F3 - Increase back to normal master volume, after using $F2.
$F4 - Gradually slow down tempo
$F5 - Gradually increase tempo

$2141
Write to play looping sound effects, seems to be mostly engine sounds

$2142
Write to play some other looping sound effects

$2143
Write to play non-looping sound effects like lasers and menu sfx
